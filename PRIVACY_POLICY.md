# Privacy Policy for FamilyHub

**Last Updated: November 10, 2025**

## Introduction

FamilyHub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using FamilyHub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use FamilyHub, we collect:

- **Email Address**: Used for account creation and authentication
- **Display Name**: Your chosen name displayed to family members
- **Profile Information**: Optional profile details you choose to share
- **Password**: Securely encrypted and stored (if using email/password authentication)

### User-Generated Content

- **Posts**: Text, photos, and other content you share with your family
- **Kanban Boards**: Task boards, columns, and task details you create
- **Tasks**: Task descriptions, assignments, and status updates
- **Comments**: Comments you make on posts and boards
- **Photos**: Images you upload to share with family members

### Family Information

- **Family Relationships**: Information about your family structure and connections
- **Family Member IDs**: References to other family members you're connected with
- **Sharing Preferences**: Your privacy settings for posts, boards, and content

### Authentication Data

- **Google Sign-In**: If you use Google Sign-In, we receive your name and email address from Google
- **Sign in with Apple**: If you use Sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID credential and optionally your name and email (you control what Apple shares)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app (features used, frequency of use)
- **Log Data**: Error logs and diagnostic information for troubleshooting

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored using **Google Firebase services**, which include:
- **Firebase Authentication**: Secure account management
- **Cloud Firestore**: Database for user profiles, posts, boards, and tasks
- **Firebase Storage**: Secure storage for uploaded photos and images

Firebase servers are located in the United States and comply with industry-standard security practices.

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption in Transit**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
- **Encryption at Rest**: Firebase encrypts data stored on their servers
- **Authentication**: Secure authentication using industry standards (OAuth 2.0, Firebase Auth)
- **Access Controls**: Only you and your designated family members can access your family data
- **Privacy Tiers**: Five-level privacy system gives you control over who sees your content

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- **Account Deletion**: If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days
- **Shared Content**: Content shared with family members may remain visible to them even after your account deletion, unless they also delete it

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your personal information in the app
- **Update**: Modify your profile, posts, and settings at any time
- **Delete**: Delete your posts, boards, and account
- **Export**: Request a copy of your data (contact support)
- **Privacy Settings**: Control who can see your content using our 5-tier privacy system

### Privacy Controls in FamilyHub

- **Default Sharing Settings**: Set default privacy levels for new posts and boards
- **Per-Item Privacy**: Choose privacy level for each post and board individually
- **Family Management**: Control which families you're connected with
- **Notification Settings**: Control what notifications you receive

### Children's Privacy

FamilyHub is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
